## Metacognitive Foundations
The MSV framework builds on Nelson and Narens (1990), distinguishing *monitoring* from *control*.
Importantly, we do not claim LLMs possess consciousness (Bender, 2021) but propose functionally equivalent computational proxies.

$$
CE = \alpha_1F_1(\text{logical consistency}) + \alpha_2F_2(\text{factual accuracy}) + \alpha_3F_3(\text{contextual appropriateness})
$$
